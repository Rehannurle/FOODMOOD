Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to enhance your experience

Last updated: January 2025

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better, faster, and safer experience on Food Mood.

We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze how you use our service, and improve our AI-powered mood-based recommendations.

Types of Cookies We Use

🔧 Essential Cookies

Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.

Examples: Login sessions, security tokens, basic site functionality

Duration: Session or up to 30 days

📊 Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how you use Food Mood to improve our service.

Examples: Page views, user interactions, search patterns

Duration: Up to 2 years

🎯 Preference Cookies

Purpose: Remember your mood preferences and location settings.

Examples: Saved moods, location preferences, UI settings

Duration: Up to 1 year

🤖 AI Enhancement Cookies

Purpose: Improve our AI recommendations based on your usage patterns.

Examples: Recommendation feedback, search refinements

Duration: Up to 6 months

Cookie Preferences

Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings
  • Cookie Preferences: Use the controls above to customize your Food Mood experience
  • Clear Cookies: You can delete existing cookies at any time through your browser

Note that disabling certain cookies may affect some features of Food Mood, such as personalized recommendations and saved preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

Food Mood may use services from trusted third parties that also set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding website usage patterns
  • Maps Services: For location-based features and directions
  • Payment Processors: For secure transaction processing

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll notify you of significant changes and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
