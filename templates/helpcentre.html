Getting Started

  • How does Food Mood work?
    Food Mood uses AI to analyze your current mood and preferences, then recommends restaurants, caf√©s, hotels, or dhabas that perfectly match your vibe. Simply select your mood and location to get personalized suggestions.
  • How do I create an account?
    Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation bar, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.
  • Is Food Mood free to use?
    Yes! Food Mood's basic features are completely free. We also offer premium features for enhanced personalization and exclusive recommendations.
  • Which cities are supported?
    Food Mood currently supports major cities across India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, and many more. We're constantly expanding to new locations.

Understanding Moods

  • What does each mood mean?
    Each mood represents different preferences: 'Euphoric' suggests celebratory places, 'Melancholic' finds comfort food spots, 'Romantic' recommends intimate settings, and 'Social' finds group-friendly venues.
  • How accurate are mood-based recommendations?
    Our AI considers factors like ambiance, cuisine type, pricing, and user reviews to match places with your mood. Accuracy improves as you use the app and provide feedback on recommendations.
  • Can I select multiple moods?
    Currently, you can select one primary mood per search. This helps our AI provide more focused and accurate recommendations tailored to your current state of mind.
  • What if I don't see my mood listed?
    Use the custom query field to describe your specific preferences, or choose the closest mood from our list. We're always adding new mood categories based on user feedback.

Troubleshooting

  • Location detection isn't working
    Ensure location permissions are enabled for your browser. You can also manually enter your location in the search field. Check your browser settings and allow location access for Food Mood.
  • No results showing for my area
    Try expanding your search radius or check if your location is spelled correctly. We're constantly adding new places to our database. You can also suggest venues through our feedback form.
  • App is loading slowly
    Clear your browser cache and cookies, check your internet connection, and try refreshing the page. For persistent issues, try using a different browser or device.
  • Search results seem incorrect
    Our AI learns from user feedback. Please rate your experiences and report incorrect recommendations. This helps improve future suggestions for you and other users.

Account & Privacy

  • How do I change my password?
    Go to your Profile page, click on "Account Settings," then select "Change Password." You'll need to enter your current password and choose a new one.
  • What data does Food Mood collect?
    We collect location data (when permitted), search preferences, and usage patterns to improve recommendations. All data is encrypted and used solely to enhance your experience. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
  • Can I delete my account?
    At the moment, permanent account deletion is not available, as we are collecting data to improve our services and provide a better experience. Rest assured, your data is stored securely and handled with the highest level of care and privacy.
  • How do I update my preferences?
    Visit your Profile page to update dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, budget ranges, and notification settings. Changes take effect immediately for future searches.

Places & Reviews

  • How can I add a new place?
    Use the "Suggest a Place" feature in the app or contact our support team with venue details. We verify all submissions before adding them to our database.
  • Can I leave reviews and ratings?
    Yes! After visiting a recommended place, you'll receive a prompt to rate your experience. Your reviews help improve recommendations for the community.
  • How are places verified?
    Our team verifies business information, operating hours, and contact details. We also use user feedback and official sources to keep information accurate and up-to-date.
  • Why don't I see photos for some places?
    We're constantly updating our photo database. Photos come from verified sources and user contributions. You can help by submitting photos of places you visit.
