Cookie Policy

Effective Date: AUGUST 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

Food Mood uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, provide personalized recommendations, and improve our services. We use cookies for:

2.1 Essential Cookies

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in to your account
  • Security: To protect against fraudulent activity and ensure secure connections
  • Session Management: To maintain your preferences during your visit
  • Load Balancing: To distribute traffic across our servers

2.2 Functional Cookies

  • Preferences: To remember your mood selections and search preferences
  • Location: To store your location preferences (only with your permission)
  • Language: To remember your language and regional settings
  • Accessibility: To remember accessibility options you've selected

2.3 Analytics Cookies

  • Usage Analytics: To understand how you interact with our service
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix technical issues
  • Feature Usage: To see which features are most popular
  • Search Analytics: To improve our AI recommendation algorithms

2.4 Marketing Cookies

  • Personalization: To show relevant content and recommendations
  • Social Media: To enable sharing features and social login
  • Advertising: To show relevant advertisements (if applicable)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They help us identify you during a single browsing session.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences between visits.

3.3 First-Party Cookies

These are cookies set directly by Food Mood to enhance your experience on our platform.

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by other companies whose services we use, such as:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Google Maps: For location services and mapping functionality
  • Social Media Platforms: For social login and sharing features
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing (if applicable)

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data

4.2 Cookie Consent Manager

When you first visit Food Mood, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time in your account settings

4.3 Mobile App Settings

If you're using our mobile app, you can control similar tracking technologies through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of Food Mood may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in each time you visit
  • Your preferences and search history won't be saved
  • Personalized recommendations may be less accurate
  • Some features may not function correctly

6. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different retention periods:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Preference cookies: Stored for up to 1 year
  • Analytics cookies: Stored for up to 2 years
  • Marketing cookies: Stored for up to 1 year

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll notify you of significant changes through our website or app.
